VALORANT: EPISODE 8 "NIGHTFALL"

The darkness rises. A new era begins December 6.

VALORANT Development Team | © 2025 Riot Games

Agents, prepare for the most ambitious VALORANT update yet. Episode 8: Nightfall introduces the game's first nocturnal map, a shadow-wielding Agent, and the long-awaited 5v5 Team Deathmatch mode.

Set in a moonlit Moroccan citadel, our new map "Kasbah" features dynamic lighting that changes throughout the round, offering new tactical opportunities. The shadowy corridors and open-air courtyards will test your adaptability.

Meet "Shade," our 23rd Agent, a Moroccan information broker who manipulates darkness to blind enemies and create temporary shadow paths for her team. Her ultimate ability, "Eclipse," plunges an area into complete darkness for 8 seconds.
